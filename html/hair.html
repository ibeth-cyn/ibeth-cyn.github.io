Designing An App for Scheduling Hair Styling Appointments

Introduction

This project was my first attempt at product design; thinking up a product from scratch, conducting research on its feasibility, coming up with a product strategy and prototyping the product. I also had not worked on a mobile App design project before so, this was an opportunity to challenge myself and learn from the experience.

The Brief

Build a 5-page website that represents the Tingg Brand and uses color and design to communicate to our Consumers, Businesses and developers.
